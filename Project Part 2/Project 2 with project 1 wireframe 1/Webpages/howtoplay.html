How to Play Yu-Gi-Oh!

Welcome to the world of Yu-Gi-Oh! Here's a quick guide on how to play:

Step 1: Setup

Prepare your deck, shuffle it, and draw 5 cards as your starting hand.

Step 2: Field Layout

Understand the field layout - Monster Zones, Spell & Trap Zones, and more.

Step 3: Gameplay

Learn the turn-based gameplay: Draw Phase, Standby Phase, Main Phase, Battle Phase, and End Phase.

Step 4: Winning

Victory comes by reducing your opponent's Life Points to zero or fulfilling specific win conditions.
